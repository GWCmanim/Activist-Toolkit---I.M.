Find Informative Websites

Below you will find links to the numerous websites that I used to receive statistics for this page. Click on them if you would like to receive more facts and information on pollution.

Resource 1:

https://www.lung.org/research/sota/key-findings#:~:text=The%20%22State%20of%20the%20Air,air%20is%20not%20shared%20equally.

This resource provides a concise basis of how widespread and devastating air pollution is. A few examples are provided on areas with the worst air quality and the effect air pollution has in the environment. This site displays other resources and articles for viewers to explore and further educate themselves on air pollution.

Resource 2:

https://www.genevaenvironmentnetwork.org/resources/updates/plastics-and-the-environment/

This resource is an extremely current website,published just a few days ago, that is filled with a lot of information on plastic waste. This site also provides numerous links to other informational websites and has videos to watch.

Resource 3:

https://www.nrdc.org/stories/water-pollution-everything-you-need-know

This resource provides information on the different types of water pollution, its cause, and how to solve the issue.
