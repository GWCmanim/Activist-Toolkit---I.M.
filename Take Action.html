How Can You Help?

The websites below are where you can sign petitions and find the contact information of congressmen and your local representatives.

Resource 1:

https://www.change.org

This resource is helpful because it is a free, trusted website where you can find many petitions to sign and share. In addition, this site is available to everyone ages 16 and up.

Resource 2:

https://www.house.gov/representatives/find-your-representative

This website is where you can enter your zip code and find your area's state and federal representatives, their duties in government, and their contact information.
